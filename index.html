Biography

Sundar Pichai is an Indian-American business executive and the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary, Google LLC. He was born on July 12, 1972, in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India.

Pichai joined Google in 2004 and has played a key role in the development of various successful products, including Google Chrome, Chrome OS, and Android. His leadership and vision have been instrumental in the growth and success of Google over the years.

In 2015, Pichai became the CEO of Google, and under his leadership, the company has continued to innovate and expand into new areas, such as artificial intelligence and cloud computing. He is known for his calm and thoughtful approach to decision-making and his commitment to privacy and user data protection.

One of Pichai's notable achievements is the unification of Google's operating systems under the umbrella of Chrome OS. This move has allowed for seamless integration between desktop and mobile devices, creating a more cohesive user experience.
